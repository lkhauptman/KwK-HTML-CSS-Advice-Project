    Getting reading for your
    upcoming KWK camp!

    Kode with Klossy is a two-week scholarship program for girls ages 13-18 to teach them the basics of coding.

    What languages will I learn?

    For the first week, you will learn Ruby on Rails. After the first week, you will learn the basics of CSS, HTML, and Sinatra, so that you can create your very own web app.

    What does a typical day look like?

    You will arrive at camp by 9 a.m. and start with a warmup activity. Throughout the day, you'll learn new concepts, listen to (fun!) lessons, do labs, and enjoy team-building activities. Lunch is usually around 12. Read more about lunch below.

    What do I need bring, and do I have to have any prior experience?

    If you prefer to bring your own laptop, you are encouraged to do so. However, laptops are also provided through the KWK camp. Just let us know beforehand! You can also bring a notebook to take notes, if that helps you. No prior experience is necessary.

    Do I need to bring my own lunch?

    All food is provided to you through the Kode with Klossy camp. Delicious and healthy snacks are also offered throughout the day. If you have any allergies or dietary restrictions, let Kode with Klossy know on the confirmation form, and we will accomodate.
